FAQ

Kingdoms SMP is a factions-based Minecraft server where players can team up, build kingdoms, and conquer the world.

Here's a list of some frequently asked questions about our server:

  • What is a factions-based server?

    A factions-based server is a Minecraft server where players can join together to form groups called "factions." Factions can work together to build kingdoms, gather resources, and defend against other factions.

  • How do I join the server?

    To join the server, you can follow the instructions on the "How to Join" page. You will need to download Minecraft and have a valid account in order to connect to the server.

  • What is the objective of the server?

    The objective of the server is to build and conquer. You can team up with other players to build your own kingdom, gather resources, and defeat other factions. The ultimate goal is to become the strongest and most dominant kingdom on the server.

How to Join

Joining the Kingdoms SMP server is easy. Simply log in with your Minecraft account, and you'll be ready to start playing and building your own kingdom. If you don't have a Minecraft account, you can create one for free on the official Minecraft website.
